Featured Brand

Airstone Compressors — Built for Industrial Reliability

Airstone rotary screw air compressors are available in fixed-speed and variable-speed drive configurations, engineered for demanding industrial environments. Radiant Engineering supplies and supports Airstone equipment across Southern Johor — from quotation and installation through to full lifecycle maintenance.

Fixed Speed

HB Series

The HB Series is a proven, reliable direct-driven rotary screw compressor designed for facilities with consistent air demand. Built for low maintenance and long service life, it delivers steady output with minimal operator intervention.

  • Constant-speed drive for stable, predictable output
  • Wide power range to suit different facility sizes
  • Robust airend with long service intervals
  • Simple controls and easy maintenance access
  • Ideal for production facilities, workshops, and factories
Variable Speed Drive

PMC Series VSD

The PMC Series uses a high-efficiency IE4 permanent magnet synchronous motor with variable speed drive to match motor speed precisely to air demand in real time — eliminating the energy waste of constant-speed operation and reducing electricity costs significantly.

  • Energy savings of over 20% vs fixed-speed models
  • Soft-start reduces electrical stress on startup
  • Fewer pressure fluctuations for sensitive processes
  • Smart controller with performance monitoring
  • Best suited for variable or fluctuating air demand
