What We Do

Our Services

From emergency 24/7 breakdown response to structured preventive maintenance, full overhaul, and spare parts supply — Radiant Engineering provides the complete range of industrial air compressor services that factories and businesses in Southern Johor depend on.

Full Service Range

Industrial Compressor Services

Whether you need an emergency repair, a scheduled maintenance programme, a full overhaul, or parts — we handle all of it. One local contact for your entire compressed air operation.

Compressor Service & 24/7 Repair

On-site fault diagnosis and emergency breakdown repair for industrial air compressors — available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. When your compressor goes down, we respond quickly and arrive prepared to fix it.

  • 24/7 emergency callout — any time, any day
  • On-site fault diagnosis and repair
  • Multi-brand compressor capability
  • Common parts carried for fast turnaround

Preventive Maintenance (PMC)

Scheduled maintenance programmes built around your operations to prevent breakdowns before they happen. Keeping your compressed air system on a regular service cycle extends lifespan and reduces unplanned downtime.

  • Scheduled programme tailored to your operations
  • Full inspection, lubrication, and system check
  • Filter, belt, oil, and consumable replacement
  • Service records provided after every visit
Learn More

Overhaul & Reconditioning

Full compressor overhaul to restore ageing or heavily-used units to reliable working condition. A cost-effective alternative to full replacement — we strip down, inspect, replace worn components, and return the unit to service.

  • Complete strip-down and inspection
  • Worn component replacement as required
  • Performance testing post-overhaul
  • Cost-effective vs purchasing a replacement unit
Learn More

Spare Parts & Consumables

Compressor spare parts and service consumables for a range of brands. We stock common items and can source specific parts — supplied to in-house maintenance teams, PMC programmes, and emergency repairs.

  • Filters, oil separators, belts, and seals
  • Service kits for common compressor brands
  • Supplied for maintenance, PMC, and repair
  • Multi-brand parts sourcing capability

Why Choose Us

Why Factories Trust Radiant

Industrial customers in Southern Johor choose Radiant because we respond fast, work on what they already have, and keep the service practical — not oversold.

24/7 Breakdown Response

A compressor failure can stop an entire production line. We provide emergency callout support around the clock — no office-hours-only restrictions — because downtime costs more than an urgent repair callout.

Based in Southern Johor

We are locally based — not a national provider dispatching from another state. Shorter travel distance means faster site visits, better technician availability, and a team that understands the local industrial environment.

Multi-Brand Capability

We service a wide range of compressor brands and models — you are not limited to a single manufacturer's service network. If you run mixed or ageing equipment, we can work on it without requiring a separate specialist for each brand.

Structured PMC Programmes

Our preventive maintenance contracts are built around your production schedule and compressor hours — not a generic calendar interval. Structured, documented, and tracked so nothing is missed and you have records when you need them.
