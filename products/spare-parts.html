What We Stock

Parts & Consumables We Carry

From scheduled consumables to overhaul-grade components — here's a broad overview of what we stock and can source for your compressor.

  • Oil Separators

    OEM and compatible oil separator elements for rotary screw compressors — critical for maintaining air quality and protecting the airend from oil re-ingestion.

  • Air & Oil Filters

    Inlet air filter elements and inline oil filters — essential consumables for scheduled maintenance to maintain airend efficiency and extend service life.

  • Compressor Oil

    Synthetic and semi-synthetic compressor lubricants for rotary screw units — correct viscosity grades for different compressor brands and operating environments.

  • Drive Belts

    V-belts and poly-V belts for belt-driven compressors — genuine and compatible options for all common compressor drive configurations.

  • Bearings

    Airend and motor bearings — including deep groove ball bearings, angular contact bearings, and roller bearings for scheduled replacement during overhaul.

  • Valve Kits

    Intake and check valve kits, minimum pressure valve assemblies, and thermostatic valve bypass components for major compressor brands.

  • Thermostatic Valves

    Temperature-controlled bypass valves that regulate oil circuit temperature — critical for preventing thermal cutout and maintaining correct operating temperature.

  • Solenoids & Sensors

    Inlet solenoid valves, pressure transducers, temperature sensors, and electronic control components for compressor controller systems.
