Complete Your System

Compressor Accessories

A compressor alone is only part of the compressed air system. Dryers, filters, separators, regulators, and piping are critical to delivering clean, dry, correctly pressurised air to your equipment and processes. Radiant Engineering supplies and installs the accessories that complete your system.

Why Accessories Matter

Protect Your Equipment. Protect Your Product.

Untreated compressed air carries moisture, oil aerosols, and particulates from the atmosphere and the compression process itself. Without proper treatment, these contaminants damage pneumatic tools, corrode pipework, contaminate products, and cause premature compressor wear.

The right accessories — correctly specified and installed — deliver clean, dry compressed air at the right pressure, protecting your investment and ensuring process consistency. Radiant Engineering can assess your system and recommend exactly what's needed.

What We Supply

Accessories We Carry

From moisture treatment through to distribution piping, we supply the accessories needed to build a complete, reliable compressed air system.

  • Refrigerant Air Dryers

    Remove moisture from compressed air before it reaches your equipment. Prevents corrosion, contamination, and product spoilage in moisture-sensitive processes.

  • Inline Air Filters

    Coalescing and particulate filters to remove oil aerosols, dust, and solid particles — protecting downstream tools, valves, and pneumatic equipment.

  • Oil/Water Separators

    Separate compressor oil from condensate before disposal — required for environmental compliance and responsible compressed air system management.

  • Pressure Regulators & Gauges

    Downstream pressure control and monitoring equipment to set and maintain the correct working pressure for different tools and process requirements.

  • Air Receiver Tanks

    Buffer storage for compressed air — reduces compressor cycling frequency, smooths pressure variations, and provides reserve capacity for peak demand periods.

  • Solenoid Valves

    Electrically operated valves for automated compressed air control — used in pneumatic circuits, drainage systems, and process automation applications.

  • Auto Drain Valves

    Automatic condensate drainage to keep receivers, dryers, and filter bowls free of accumulated moisture without manual intervention.

  • Piping & Fittings

    Aluminium and galvanised compressed air piping systems and push-fit fittings for clean, leak-free distribution networks within your facility.
