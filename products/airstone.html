Fixed Speed

CSM Series — Reliable, Constant-Output Performance

The CSM Series is a straightforward, dependable rotary screw compressor built for facilities with steady, consistent air demand. It delivers reliable output without complexity — ideal for production environments where uptime is the priority.

  • Rotary Screw Airend

    High-efficiency twin-screw airend for continuous duty operation with minimal pulsation and vibration.

  • Fixed-Speed Drive Motor

    Constant-speed operation delivers stable, predictable compressed air output suited to steady demand.

  • Integrated Oil Separation System

    Built-in oil separator and filtration minimises oil carryover and keeps downstream equipment clean.

  • Simple, Reliable Controls

    Straightforward control panel with pressure setpoint, run/stop, and basic fault indication — easy to operate and maintain.

  • Long Service Intervals

    Designed for extended maintenance intervals to reduce servicing frequency and keep operating costs low.

  • Compact, Self-Contained Package

    Compressor, motor, coolers, and separator housed in a single acoustic enclosure — minimal installation footprint.

  • Low Noise Operation

    Acoustic canopy and vibration-isolated mounting keep noise levels acceptable for workshop and factory environments.

  • Suitable for Continuous Duty

    Rated for prolonged operation without thermal cutout — suited to production lines that need constant air supply throughout the working day.

Variable Speed Drive

DRB Series VSD — Smart Energy, Matched to Demand

The DRB VSD Series uses variable frequency drive technology to match compressor output precisely to actual air demand in real time — eliminating energy waste during low-demand periods and reducing electricity costs by up to 35% versus constant-speed units.

  • Variable Frequency Drive (VFD)

    Motor speed continuously adjusts to match actual air consumption — no energy wasted on load/unload cycling.

  • Up to 35% Energy Savings

    Significant reduction in electricity consumption versus fixed-speed alternatives, especially in facilities with variable air demand.

  • Soft-Start Technology

    Gradual motor acceleration reduces electrical stress and mechanical shock on startup — extends motor and airend life.

  • Stable System Pressure

    Maintains tighter pressure band than fixed-speed units, reducing pressure drops and improving quality for sensitive downstream processes.

  • Smart Controller with Monitoring

    Built-in controller displays real-time operating data — pressure, temperature, power draw, and service intervals — for informed maintenance decisions.

  • Reduced Noise at Partial Load

    Motor and fan speed reduce proportionally at lower demand, making the DRB VSD noticeably quieter during off-peak hours.

  • Extended Component Life

    Fewer start-stop cycles and lower average operating temperatures result in reduced wear on bearings, seals, and the airend.

  • Ideal for Variable Demand Profiles

    Best suited to facilities where compressed air usage fluctuates across shifts, production cycles, or seasons.
