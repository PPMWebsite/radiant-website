About Radiant Engineering

Supporting Johor's Industrial Operations

Radiant Engineering & Trading Sdn. Bhd. is a Southern Johor-based compressed air specialist, supporting factories, workshops, and industrial operations with the equipment, servicing, and parts they need to keep their compressed air systems running reliably.
