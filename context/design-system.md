Purpose:
Provide brand and design intent for Claude to infer the best-fit website design for Radiant Engineering.

Brand Consistency:
- Stay visually consistent with the live Radiant websites
- Preserve the existing corporate feel, colors, and typography direction from the live sites
- Do not replace the brand identity with a generic template look

Design Objective:
- Build a more polished, credible, and conversion-focused website than the current live site
- Fit the expectations of industrial B2B buyers, maintenance teams, and factory decision makers
- Improve clarity, hierarchy, usability, and product visibility

Business Context to Respect:
- Radiant is in industrial air compressors, servicing, preventive maintenance, overhaul work, spare parts, equipment support, and related compressed air solutions
- Service credibility and product visibility both matter
- Products should receive stronger visibility because they are commercially important

Design Intent:
- Let Claude determine the most suitable layout, visual hierarchy, spacing, section flow, and CTA placement
- Use the factual business context and existing live-site brand feel as the main reference
- Prioritize strong navigation, clear service explanation, product-category discoverability, and repeated conversion opportunities

Conversion Intent:
- Keep WhatsApp as the main CTA path
- Support Call, Get a Quote, and Request Service / Site Visit as secondary actions
- Make it easy for visitors to move from browsing into enquiry

SEO Intent:
- Build pages that are useful for industrial search intent
- Support strong content structure, clear headings, internal linking, and category visibility
- Avoid thin or purely decorative sections

Visual Direction:
- Professional
- Industrial
- Trustworthy
- Practical
- Clean
- Structured
- More visually appealing than the current live site without losing relevance to the industry

Imagery Direction:
- Compressors
- Components
- Parts
- Service / maintenance work
- Industrial environments
- Product-category visuals
- Avoid generic decorative stock imagery
