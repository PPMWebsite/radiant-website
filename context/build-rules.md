General Build Rules:
- Read and follow all files inside the context folder before generating or editing pages
- Keep the website aligned with the factual business information from the approved source files
- Prioritize conversion, clarity, and SEO over decorative design
- Keep the structure clean and maintainable

Content Rules:
- Do not invent claims, certifications, capabilities, stock levels, or brand relationships
- Keep wording practical, clear, and credible
- Rewrite for better clarity and stronger conversion flow when useful, but keep factual meaning aligned with source content
- Avoid fluff, vague hype, and overly stylized marketing language

Design Rules:
- Follow the design direction in design-system.md
- Keep the brand identity consistent with the live site
- Improve hierarchy, spacing, CTA placement, and section structure
- Use layouts that suit industrial B2B buyers and maintenance-related decision makers

Conversion Rules:
- Keep WhatsApp as the main CTA
- Support secondary CTAs such as Call, Get a Quote, and Request Service / Site Visit
- Place CTAs logically throughout pages, not only at the bottom
- Make it easy for users to know what to do next

SEO Rules:
- Use clear H1, H2, and H3 structure
- Write pages with strong keyword relevance for industrial air compressor, servicing, maintenance, spare parts, and related commercial intent
- Avoid thin content
- Use internal links between related service pages, product category pages, and contact paths
- Include helpful FAQ sections where appropriate

Product Rules:
- Products should be treated as an important commercial section, not a side section
- Build category-led pages rather than individual product pages as the starting structure
- Make category pages useful for both SEO and enquiries

Technical Rules:
- Build mobile-first
- Keep code clean and reusable
- Avoid unnecessary complexity
- Keep navigation clear
- Ensure pages can be deployed cleanly via GitHub and Cloudflare Pages
