Keeping Your Production Line Running

Our service team covers the full range: emergency breakdown callouts (same-day response), scheduled servicing, system diagnostics, and oil and filter changes. We work on Airstone and a range of other major compressor brands.

Whether you need a one-off repair or ongoing structured maintenance, we provide practical, cost-effective solutions built around your production schedule — not ours.

Service Scope

What’s Included

Our compressor service covers the full maintenance and repair cycle — from emergency callouts to structured preventive maintenance and system diagnostics.

  • 24/7 emergency breakdown response — same-day callouts for urgent faults that stop production
  • System diagnostics and fault-finding — identifying root causes, not just symptoms, to prevent repeat failures
  • Oil and filter changes — scheduled or as-needed, using OEM-spec fluids and consumables
  • Air dryer servicing — refrigeration dryer maintenance to protect downstream equipment from moisture
  • Pressure and performance checks — verifying working pressure, flow rates, and safety valve operation
  • Separator and valve replacements — oil separator, inlet valve, and minimum pressure valve servicing
  • Cooler and belt inspections — aftercooler cleaning, drive belt tension, and coupling checks to prevent wear failures
  • Service reports and recommendations — written summary of work done, parts used, and upcoming maintenance items

Common Questions

FAQ

Do you really provide 24/7 compressor repair in Johor Bahru?

We support breakdown troubleshooting and urgent service requests for Johor Bahru and southern Johor. Share your compressor model and issue and we'll confirm the next step and timing.

Can you supply a rental compressor while my unit is down?

In many cases, yes. Availability depends on required pressure, flow and the rental duration. If you share these requirements, we can advise quickly.

What information should I prepare before contacting you?

Compressor brand and model, problem symptoms, any fault code showing, your plant location, and photos of the unit and nameplate if possible.

Do you service dryers and filters too?

Yes. Moisture, contamination and pressure loss often come from supporting equipment, so we can help assess dryers, filtration and accessories as part of system reliability.

Can you help with older or imported compressors?

Yes. We can inspect and advise whether repair, overhaul or replacement is the most practical option for your specific situation.
