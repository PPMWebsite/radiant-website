Spare Parts & Consumables

Don't let a missing part extend your downtime. Radiant Engineering supplies OEM-compatible spare parts and consumables for all major compressor brands — with local stock, expert advice on correct part selection, and fast delivery across Southern Johor.

What We Supply

The Right Part, From a Team That Knows Compressors

Sourcing the right spare part quickly is critical when a compressor is down. Wrong parts, long lead times, or compatibility guesswork all extend downtime and risk further damage.

Radiant Engineering supplies a comprehensive range of consumables and mechanical parts for the major compressor brands operating in Johor. Because our team also services and overhauls compressors, we know exactly which part fits which unit — and whether a direct OEM replacement or a compatible alternative is the right choice for your situation.

We can advise on part selection based on your compressor brand, model, and serial number, source fast from our network, and deliver direct or hand-deliver with on-site fitment if needed.

Parts Range

Parts & Consumables We Supply

We cover the full range of service consumables and mechanical components across all major compressor brands.

  • Oil separator elements — the most commonly replaced consumable; we stock or can fast-source for Airstone and other major brands
  • Oil filters and air inlet filters — OEM-spec filtration elements for scheduled replacement at correct service intervals
  • Compressor lubricating oil — brand-matched synthetic and semi-synthetic compressor oil in correct viscosity grades for your equipment
  • Drive belts and belt kits — V-belts and poly-V belts for belt-driven compressor models; supplied with tensioning specs
  • Bearing kits — main drive bearings and motor bearings for major models; critical for overhaul or preventive replacement
  • Inlet valve and minimum pressure valve kits — service kits for common valve types; includes seats, springs, and seals
  • Thermostatic bypass valves — replacement cartridges and complete units for oil temperature regulation
  • Solenoid valves and electronic components — pressure transducers, temperature sensors, solenoid coils, and control board components for common models
