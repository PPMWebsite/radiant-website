Who We Are

About Radiant Engineering

Southern Johor's dedicated industrial air compressor specialist — supplying, servicing, and supporting factories and industrial operations with compressed air solutions they can rely on.

Radiant Engineering & Trading Sdn. Bhd.

Your Local Compressed Air Partner in Southern Johor

Radiant Engineering & Trading Sdn. Bhd. is an industrial air compressor company based in Southern Johor, Malaysia. We support factories, workshops, and industrial businesses with a full range of compressed air services and equipment — from routine preventive maintenance and emergency breakdown repairs to compressor supply, overhaul, spare parts, and rental support.

We work with what our customers already have on site. Our service team handles a broad range of compressor brands and models, making us a practical partner for operations running mixed or ageing equipment. For new installations, we supply Ceccato compressors from Italy — a brand known for reliability in industrial environments.

Our focus is on keeping your compressed air system running. We respond quickly to breakdowns, offer structured preventive maintenance programmes, and maintain stock of common parts and consumables so that downtime stays short.

Our Approach

What We Stand For

Industrial buyers need reliable partners, not sales pitches. These principles guide how we work with every customer.

Fast Response When It Matters

A compressor breakdown can stop production. We offer 24/7 emergency response because we understand what downtime costs. When you call, we move quickly — not on the next available slot.

Locally Based, Locally Committed

We are not a national chain dispatching from another state. We are based in Southern Johor and serve the local industrial market — which means faster site visits, better availability, and a team that knows the area.

Practical, Honest Recommendations

We give customers clear, practical advice on what their compressor system needs — not unnecessary upsells. Whether it's a service call, a part replacement, or a new unit, the recommendation fits the situation.

One Source for Service & Supply

Dealing with multiple vendors for servicing, parts, and equipment adds friction. We handle all of it — servicing, maintenance contracts, spare parts, accessories, new compressors, and rental — from one local point of contact.
